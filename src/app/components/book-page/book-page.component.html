"Don't Make Me Think" by Steve Krug is a practical guide on web usability, emphasizing the importance of intuitive design to enhance user experience. It advocates for simplicity, clear navigation, and reducing cognitive load for users. Key principles include prioritizing user-centered design and conducting regular usability testing to identify and fix issues.
